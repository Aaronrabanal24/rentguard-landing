FAQ

How are deposits released?

Funds are released by licensed escrow partners after move-out approval or neutral mediation.

What does it cost?

Free for tenants during launch. Landlords pay a small escrow fee (1–2% of the deposit).

Is it legal?

Yes. We use state-compliant leases and licensed escrow providers.

When do you launch?

California launch in Q2 2025. Expansion to other states will follow based on demand.
